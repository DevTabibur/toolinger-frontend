How to Check Word Count in Google Docs

Google Docs also makes it easy to check your word count, but the shortcut is unknown to many users. Do the following:

  • Open your document in Google Docs.
  • Click on Tools in the top menu.
  • Choose Word count.
  • A box will pop up displaying words, characters (including and excluding spaces), and pages.

Press Ctrl + Shift + C (Windows) or Command + Shift + C (Mac) to bring up word count directly.

This is convenient for essays, research, or any project where you must monitor word count. For more detail, copy and paste your text into our online Word Counter for Google Docs and get more information like reading time and keyword density.

How to Count Words in Microsoft Word

If you're using Microsoft Word, do the following to view your word count:

  • Open your document.
  • Look at the bottom left of your screen.
  • You'll see "Word Count" there.

For a longer breakdown:

  • Click Review in the top menu.
  • Then click Word Count to see pages, words, characters (with and without spaces), paragraphs, and lines.

But if you're looking for pro statistics like speaking time, readability, or keyword analysis, copy your text into our tool and get more than what Word provides.

How to Count Characters in an Image

Ever wanted a word counter from images or screenshots? Since pictures don’t contain direct text, you’ll need OCR (Optical Character Recognition) software to extract the text first.

Workflow:

  • Use an OCR tool (like Google Keep, OnlineOCR, or built-in phone scanners).
  • Copy the extracted text.
  • Paste it into our Word Counter online to analyze the words, characters, and sentences.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to see word count on Google Docs?

Go to Tools > Word Count in Google Docs to view your word count. A dialog box will appear with the word count, characters (with and without spaces), and pages. Alternatively, use the shortcut Ctrl + Shift + C (Windows) or Command + Shift + C (Mac).

How to show word count on Google Docs?

If you want the word count to continue to be shown while you type:

  • Go to Tools > Word Count.
  • Select the "Display word count while typing" checkbox.
  • The word count will appear dynamic in the bottom left of your document.

Can I check word count in Microsoft Word?

Yes. The word count is located in the bottom left corner of the screen in Microsoft Word. For detailed statistics, go to Review > Word Count, and you will see words, characters, paragraphs, and lines.

How do I count words from a picture?

Since a picture doesn’t have text data, use an OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tool to extract the text first. Then paste it into our Word Counter online to see the total words and characters.

Does your tool support other languages like Urdu?

Yes! Our Urdu word counter works the same way as English. Paste your Urdu text, and you’ll instantly see the word and character count.
