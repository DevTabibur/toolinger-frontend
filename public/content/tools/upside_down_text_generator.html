The software uses special Unicode characters that look like inverted versions of normal letters and symbols. As you type text into the input box, it automatically replaces your words with these flipped characters, giving the illusion that your text is upside down. Is flipped text the same as reversed text?

No. Flipped text flips letters to their reverse side versions (like flipping "p" to "d"). Reversed text reverses the letter sequence without flipping them.
